# Greed Island Cards

## Topic: Card System
Summary:
Greed Island uses a Nen-based card system. Players collect restricted-slot cards and use spell cards to move, copy, inspect, heal, or leave the game.

## Card: Book
Effect:
Book opens a player's binder and manages collected cards.

## Card: Gain
Effect:
Gain turns a card into its actual item or effect.

## Card: Leave
Effect:
Leave lets a player exit Greed Island.

## Card: Accompany
Effect:
Accompany transports a player and companions to another player or location according to game rules.

## Card: Angel's Breath / Archangel's Breath
Effect:
Angel's Breath is a powerful healing card.

## Card: Paladin's Necklace
Effect:
Paladin's Necklace can protect against or identify certain card effects depending on game rules.

## Restricted Slot Cards
Restricted-slot cards are rare cards needed to complete the game. Strategy often depends on collecting, trading, protecting, or stealing them.
