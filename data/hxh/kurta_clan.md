# Kurta Clan

## Group: Kurta Clan
Summary:
The Kurta Clan is Kurapika's clan. Their eyes turn scarlet under strong emotion, making them valuable to collectors.

Key facts:
- The clan was massacred.
- Their Scarlet Eyes were stolen.
- Kurapika is the last known survivor.
- The massacre is tied to Kurapika's hatred of the Phantom Troupe.

Common questions:
- Why does Kurapika hate the Phantom Troupe?
- What are the Scarlet Eyes?
- What happened to the Kurta Clan?
