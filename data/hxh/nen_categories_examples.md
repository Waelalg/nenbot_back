# Nen Categories and Examples

## Category: Enhancement
Definition:
Enhancers strengthen bodies or objects. They are often direct fighters.
Examples:
- Gon Freecss: Jajanken Rock.
- Uvogin: raw physical and aura power.
- Netero has enhancement-like martial discipline even though his full ability is more complex.

## Category: Transmutation
Definition:
Transmuters change aura properties.
Examples:
- Killua Zoldyck: electricity-like aura.
- Hisoka Morow: Bungee Gum.
- Biscuit Krueger: advanced Nen use and training.

## Category: Emission
Definition:
Emitters project aura away from the body.
Examples:
- Leorio: remote punch.
- Razor: powerful emitted aura in Greed Island.
- Pokkle: arrow-like aura use.

## Category: Conjuration
Definition:
Conjurers create objects from aura.
Examples:
- Kurapika: chains.
- Shizuku: Blinky vacuum.
- Kite: Crazy Slots weapons.

## Category: Manipulation
Definition:
Manipulators control people, objects, or conditions.
Examples:
- Illumi: needle manipulation.
- Shalnark: antenna-based control.

## Category: Specialization
Definition:
Specialists use abilities that do not fit normal categories.
Examples:
- Chrollo: Skill Hunter.
- Neon: fortune-telling ability.
- Kurapika: Emperor Time when Scarlet Eyes are active.
