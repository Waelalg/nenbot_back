# Phantom Troupe

## Topic: Phantom Troupe
Type: Criminal group
Aliases: Phantom Troupe, Phantom Troop, Spiders, Genei Ryodan
Related: Chrollo Lucilfer, Kurapika, Kurta Clan, Yorknew City, Meteor City

Summary:
The Phantom Troupe is a notorious criminal group from Meteor City. Its members are called Spiders and are led by Chrollo Lucilfer.

Key facts:
- The group is feared for strength, loyalty, and brutality.
- Kurapika hunts them because of the Kurta Clan massacre.
- They are central to the Yorknew City arc.
- Chrollo's leadership and Skill Hunter ability make the group highly dangerous.

## Major Members
Important members include Chrollo, Feitan, Machi, Nobunaga, Phinks, Franklin, Shizuku, Shalnark, Bonolenov, Pakunoda, Uvogin, Kortopi, and later Kalluto.

## Yorknew Role
The Troupe attacks the mafia auction and becomes the focus of Kurapika's revenge.

## Meteor City
Meteor City is connected to the Troupe's origin and identity.

## Group identity
The Troupe members are symbolized by the Spider emblem and usually act more like a criminal family than a simple gang. Their internal loyalty is part of what makes them so dangerous.

## Conflict with Kurapika
Kurapika targets the group because of the Kurta Clan massacre and the theft of the Scarlet Eyes. This turns the Phantom Troupe into one of the core forces in Kurapika's revenge storyline.
