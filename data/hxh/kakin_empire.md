# Kakin Empire

## Group: Kakin Empire
Summary:
The Kakin Empire sponsors the Black Whale voyage and the Dark Continent expedition while its princes compete in a deadly Succession Contest.

Key facts:
- The royal family has multiple princes.
- Princes are tied to Guardian Spirit Beasts.
- The Black Whale carries Kakin royalty, bodyguards, civilians, and expedition forces.
- Kurapika becomes involved as a bodyguard for Queen Oito and Prince Woble.
