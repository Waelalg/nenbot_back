# Groups and Factions

## Hunter Association
The Hunter Association licenses professional Hunters and influences exploration, security, politics, research, and dangerous missions. Isaac Netero served as chairman for many years. After his death, the Association holds an election involving the Zodiacs and other Hunters.

## Phantom Troupe
The Phantom Troupe, also called the Spiders, is a notorious criminal group from Meteor City. Chrollo Lucilfer leads the group. Members include Feitan, Machi, Nobunaga, Phinks, Shizuku, Franklin, Shalnark, Bonolenov, and others. The Troupe is tied to the massacre of the Kurta Clan and is central to Kurapika's revenge.

## Zoldyck Family
The Zoldyck family is a legendary assassin family living on Kukuroo Mountain. Important members include Zeno, Silva, Kikyo, Illumi, Milluki, Killua, Alluka, and Kalluto. The family trains children harshly and treats assassination as a profession.

## Chimera Ants
Chimera Ants are dangerous creatures that inherit traits from what the Queen consumes. The Chimera Ant hierarchy includes the Queen, Squadron Leaders, soldiers, Royal Guards, and the King. Meruem is the King, and Neferpitou, Shaiapouf, and Menthuthuyoupi are his Royal Guards.

## Zodiacs
The Zodiacs are elite Hunters associated with the leadership of the Hunter Association. They become especially important during the chairman election after Netero's death.
