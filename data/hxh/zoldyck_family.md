# Zoldyck Family

## Topic: Zoldyck Family
Type: Assassin family
Aliases: Zoldyck, Zoldick, Zoldyck Family
Related: Killua, Illumi, Silva, Zeno, Alluka, Nanika, Kukuroo Mountain

Summary:
The Zoldyck family is a legendary assassin family based on Kukuroo Mountain. Killua is born into this family but wants freedom from its control.

Key facts:
- Silva is Killua's father and family head.
- Zeno is Killua's grandfather and a master assassin.
- Illumi manipulates Killua with needles and psychological control.
- Alluka and Nanika are hidden because of Nanika's dangerous wish-granting power.
- The family treats assassination as a profession.

## Killua's Conflict
Killua wants friendship and self-determination, especially through his bond with Gon and his care for Alluka.

## Family Rules
The family values strength, obedience, secrecy, and assassination skills.
