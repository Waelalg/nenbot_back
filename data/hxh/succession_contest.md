# Succession Contest

## Topic: Succession Contest
Type: Manga arc
Aliases: Succession Contest, Succession War
Related: Kakin Empire, Black Whale, Guardian Spirit Beasts, Kurapika, Dark Continent

Summary:
The Succession Contest is a deadly conflict among Kakin princes aboard the Black Whale. The contest occurs during the voyage connected to the Dark Continent expedition.

Key facts:
- Kakin princes compete to become the next ruler.
- Guardian Spirit Beasts are Nen entities tied to the princes.
- Kurapika works as a bodyguard for Queen Oito and Prince Woble.
- The conflict mixes politics, assassination, Nen mysteries, and survival.
- The Phantom Troupe and Hisoka also become relevant aboard the ship.

## Black Whale
The Black Whale carries Kakin royalty, civilians, bodyguards, Hunters, and expedition forces.

## Kurapika's Role
Kurapika protects Woble while investigating Nen threats and pursuing his own goals.
