# Villains and Antagonists

## Entity: Hisoka Morow
Type: Antagonist / rival
Summary:
Hisoka is not aligned with a heroic side. He seeks exciting battles and manipulates situations to fight strong opponents.

## Entity: Chrollo Lucilfer
Type: Antagonist / group leader
Summary:
Chrollo leads the Phantom Troupe. He is calm, philosophical, and dangerous because he combines loyalty to the Troupe with strategic cruelty.

## Entity: Phantom Troupe Members
Type: Criminal group
Summary:
Major Troupe members include Chrollo, Nobunaga, Feitan, Machi, Phinks, Shalnark, Franklin, Pakunoda, Shizuku, Bonolenov, Kortopi, Uvogin, and Kalluto. They are central antagonists in Yorknew City.

## Entity: Genthru
Type: Greed Island antagonist
Summary:
Genthru, also called the Bomber, threatens other players in Greed Island and uses explosive Nen conditions.

## Entity: Meruem
Type: Chimera Ant King
Summary:
Meruem begins as the central threat of the Chimera Ant arc but develops complexity through Komugi.

## Entity: Royal Guards
Type: Chimera Ant antagonists
Summary:
Neferpitou, Shaiapouf, and Menthuthuyoupi protect Meruem and serve as the main obstacles during the palace invasion.

## Entity: Illumi Zoldyck
Type: Antagonistic family figure
Summary:
Illumi controls and pressures Killua, making him one of Killua's main personal antagonists.

## Entity: Kakin Succession Conflict
Type: Political conflict
Summary:
The Kakin Succession Contest creates many antagonistic forces because princes, bodyguards, and Nen beasts compete for survival and power.
