# Story Timeline

## Progression: From Hunter Exam to Dark Continent
Summary:
Hunter x Hunter begins with Gon leaving Whale Island to become a Hunter and find Ging. The story expands from personal adventure into organized crime, Nen combat, biological catastrophe, political succession, and Dark Continent exploration.

Key sequence:
1. Hunter Exam: Gon meets Killua, Kurapika, and Leorio.
2. Zoldyck Family: Killua's family background is introduced.
3. Heavens Arena: Gon and Killua learn Nen.
4. Yorknew City: Kurapika confronts the Phantom Troupe.
5. Greed Island: Gon and Killua train and follow Ging's clues.
6. Chimera Ant: Meruem and the Royal Guards threaten humanity.
7. Election: Gon recovers, Killua protects Alluka, and the Hunter Association changes leadership.
8. Dark Continent and Succession Contest: Kakin politics, Kurapika's mission, and the voyage beyond the known world begin.

## Gon Journey
Gon's journey starts as a search for Ging and becomes a test of friendship, morality, and emotional control.

## Killua Development
Killua moves from family-controlled assassin to a friend capable of making independent choices, especially through Gon and Alluka.

## Kurapika Revenge Path
Kurapika's story is driven by the Kurta Clan massacre, the Scarlet Eyes, and his conflict with the Phantom Troupe.
