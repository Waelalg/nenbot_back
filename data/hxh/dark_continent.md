# Dark Continent

## Topic: Dark Continent
Summary:
The Dark Continent is a massive and dangerous land beyond the known world. It contains unknown creatures, disasters, resources, and risks far beyond normal Hunter missions.

Key facts:
- Beyond Netero wants to lead an expedition.
- The Kakin Empire becomes involved.
- The voyage connects to the Black Whale and Succession Contest.
- The Dark Continent represents exploration, ambition, and catastrophic danger.

Common questions:
- What is the Dark Continent?
- Why is the Dark Continent dangerous?
- Who is Beyond Netero?
