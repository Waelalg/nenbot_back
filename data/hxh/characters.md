# Hunter x Hunter Characters

## Gon Freecss
Gon Freecss is the main protagonist. He is an optimistic boy from Whale Island who becomes a Hunter to find his father, Ging Freecss. Gon is naturally direct, loyal, and reckless when his friends are threatened. His Nen affinity is Enhancement, and his signature technique is Jajanken, which channels aura into rock, paper, and scissors style attacks.

## Killua Zoldyck
Killua Zoldyck is Gon's closest friend and a member of the famous Zoldyck assassin family. He is highly intelligent, fast, and trained in assassination from childhood. Killua's Nen affinity is Transmutation, and he develops electricity-based techniques such as Godspeed. His growth focuses on gaining independence from his family's control and protecting Gon.

## Kurapika
Kurapika is the last known survivor of the Kurta Clan. His goals are to recover the stolen Scarlet Eyes and punish the Phantom Troupe. Kurapika normally belongs to Conjuration, but his Scarlet Eyes activate Emperor Time, allowing him to use all Nen categories at full efficiency. Chain Jail is restricted to Phantom Troupe members, making it extremely powerful under that condition.

## Leorio Paradinight
Leorio Paradinight is one of Gon's earliest friends. He wants to become a doctor so he can help people who cannot afford medical care. Leorio is practical, emotional, loyal, and often acts as the normal human perspective in the main group. His Nen ability is later shown as an Emission-style remote punch.

## Hisoka Morow
Hisoka Morow is a dangerous magician-like fighter who seeks strong opponents. He is unpredictable, selfish, and fascinated by the potential of Gon and Killua. His Nen affinity is Transmutation. His main ability, Bungee Gum, gives aura the properties of both rubber and gum, letting him pull, trap, and redirect targets.

## Chrollo Lucilfer
Chrollo Lucilfer is the leader of the Phantom Troupe. He is calm, intelligent, and deeply loyal to the group. His Nen ability, Skill Hunter, lets him steal and use other people's Nen abilities under specific conditions. Chrollo is one of the most strategic fighters in the series.

## Isaac Netero
Isaac Netero is the former chairman of the Hunter Association and one of the strongest Nen users shown. He is known for incredible speed, discipline, and the 100-Type Guanyin Bodhisattva ability. During the Chimera Ant arc, Netero fights Meruem and represents the peak of human martial training.

## Meruem
Meruem is the Chimera Ant King. He begins as a ruthless ruler who values strength above everything, but his relationship with Komugi changes his understanding of humanity and identity. Meruem is central to the Chimera Ant arc's themes of power, morality, and compassion.

## Biscuit Krueger
Biscuit Krueger, often called Bisky, is a veteran Hunter and Nen master. She trains Gon and Killua during Greed Island. Bisky usually appears as a small girl but can reveal a much more muscular true form. Her teaching helps Gon and Killua improve aura control, combat judgment, and Nen fundamentals.

## Kite
Kite is a Hunter and a student of Ging Freecss. He introduces Gon to the reality of Hunters and later investigates the Chimera Ant threat. His Nen ability, Crazy Slots, summons randomized weapons through a clown-like entity. Kite's fate is a major emotional trigger for Gon in the Chimera Ant arc.

## Illumi Zoldyck
Illumi Zoldyck is Killua's older brother and a manipulative assassin. He uses needle-based Manipulation Nen to control people and influence Killua's behavior. Illumi's control over Killua is an important obstacle in Killua's personal development.

## Alluka Zoldyck and Nanika
Alluka Zoldyck is a child of the Zoldyck family. Nanika is a mysterious being connected to Alluka who can grant wishes after requests are fulfilled. The power is extremely dangerous under normal conditions, but Killua has a unique bond with Alluka and Nanika.

## Zeno Zoldyck
Zeno Zoldyck is Killua's grandfather and an elite assassin. He is calm, experienced, and uses dragon-shaped Nen techniques such as Dragon Dive. Zeno participates in the operation against the Chimera Ant palace.

## Silva Zoldyck
Silva Zoldyck is Killua's father and the head of the Zoldyck family. He is a powerful assassin with great physical strength and destructive aura attacks. Silva respects strength and family discipline, but he also recognizes Killua's unusual potential.

## Feitan Portor
Feitan is a member of the Phantom Troupe. He is fast, cruel, and skilled with a sword. His Nen ability Pain Packer converts damage he receives into a counterattack, with Rising Sun being a destructive heat-based form.

## Neferpitou
Neferpitou is one of Meruem's Royal Guards. Pitou has terrifying aura, strong instincts, and medical or puppet-like Nen abilities such as Doctor Blythe and Terpsichora. Pitou's actions are central to Gon's emotional collapse in the Chimera Ant arc.

## Shaiapouf
Shaiapouf, often called Pouf, is a Royal Guard devoted to Meruem's ideal role as king. Pouf is manipulative, theatrical, and strategic. His abilities involve splitting his body into smaller units and influencing others through scales.

## Menthuthuyoupi
Menthuthuyoupi, often called Youpi, is a Royal Guard with immense physical power. Unlike Pitou and Pouf, he is not derived from humans and has a more straightforward warrior mentality. His body can transform during battle, making him extremely difficult to defeat.
