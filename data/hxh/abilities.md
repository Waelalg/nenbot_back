# Nen Abilities

## Ability: Jajanken
User: Gon Freecss
Nen category: Enhancement primarily, with variations.
Effect: Gon charges aura and releases rock, scissors, or paper attacks.
Limitation: charging takes time and can be predicted.
Arc relevance: developed through training and used in major fights.

## Ability: Godspeed
User: Killua Zoldyck
Nen category: Transmutation
Effect: Killua transmutes aura into electricity and uses it to move and react with extreme speed.
Limitation: depends on charging and managing electricity-based aura.
Arc relevance: important in the Chimera Ant arc.

## Ability: Whirlwind
User: Killua Zoldyck
Nen category: Transmutation
Effect: automates Killua's reactions with electrical aura so he can respond extremely quickly.
Limitation: depends on Killua's charged electricity and programmed reaction conditions.

## Ability: Speed of Lightning
User: Killua Zoldyck
Nen category: Transmutation
Effect: uses electrical aura to move at very high speed.

## Ability: Killua Electricity
User: Killua Zoldyck
Nen category: Transmutation
Effect: Killua gives aura electric properties for stunning, speed, and reflex control.

## Ability: Chain Jail
User: Kurapika
Nen category: Conjuration with strict condition
Effect: binds a target and forces them into Zetsu.
Limitation: Kurapika restricts it to Phantom Troupe members; breaking the vow risks his life.
Arc relevance: central to Kurapika's revenge in Yorknew City.

## Ability: Judgment Chain
User: Kurapika
Effect: places a chain around a target's heart and imposes a rule.
Limitation: tied to conditions and Kurapika's resolve.

## Ability: Dowsing Chain
User: Kurapika
Effect: helps Kurapika locate targets and detect lies or clues depending on the situation.

## Ability: Holy Chain
User: Kurapika
Effect: heals injuries using Kurapika's chain ability.

## Ability: Emperor Time
User: Kurapika
Nen category: Specialization when Scarlet Eyes are active
Effect: lets Kurapika use all Nen categories at full efficiency.
Limitation: has a severe life-span cost.

## Ability: Bungee Gum
User: Hisoka Morow
Nen category: Transmutation
Effect: gives aura the properties of both rubber and gum, letting Hisoka stretch, stick, pull, trap, or redirect targets.
Limitation: depends on placement, deception, and Hisoka's tactical use.

## Ability: Texture Surprise
User: Hisoka Morow
Effect: changes the visual appearance of thin surfaces for deception.

## Ability: Skill Hunter
User: Chrollo Lucilfer
Nen category: Specialization
Effect: steals and uses other people's Nen abilities after conditions are met.
Limitation: abilities are stored in a conjured book and require rules to use.

## Ability: 100-Type Guanyin Bodhisattva
User: Isaac Netero
Effect: manifests a giant statue that attacks with extraordinary speed.
Arc relevance: used against Meruem.

## Ability: Dragon Dive
User: Zeno Zoldyck
Effect: splits dragon-shaped aura into many falling attacks.
Arc relevance: used during the palace invasion.

## Ability: Dragon Head
User: Zeno Zoldyck
Effect: shapes aura like a dragon for movement or attack.

## Ability: Deep Purple
User: Morel Mackernasey
Effect: manipulates smoke constructs for deception, scouting, and combat.

## Ability: Smoky Jail
User: Morel Mackernasey
Effect: creates a smoke enclosure that traps targets or controls space.

## Ability: APR / Hakoware
User: Knuckle Bine
Effect: tracks aura debt. If the debt exceeds a threshold, the target is forced into Zetsu.

## Ability: Bankruptcy Chapter Seven
User: Knuckle Bine
Effect: activates after Hakoware's aura debt reaches its limit, forcing the target into Zetsu.

## Ability: Hotel Rafflesia
User: Shoot McMahon
Effect: captures or stores parts of a target through Shoot's Nen ability during combat.

## Ability: Doctor Blythe
User: Neferpitou
Effect: performs complex medical treatment.
Limitation: Pitou must stay connected and cannot freely move while using it.

## Ability: Terpsichora
User: Neferpitou
Effect: enhances puppet-like combat movement and attack readiness.

## Ability: Spiritual Message
User: Shaiapouf
Effect: uses scales and aura to read or influence emotional states.

## Ability: Youpi Transformation
User: Menthuthuyoupi
Effect: transforms body shape for combat and defense.

## Ability: Nanika Wish-Granting
User: Nanika through Alluka
Effect: grants wishes after request conditions.
Limitation: normally dangerous and tied to escalating requests, though Killua has a special relationship with Nanika.

## Ability: Greed Island Cards
User: Greed Island players
Effect: cards create game effects through Nen systems. Spell cards can move players, heal, copy, or reveal information.

## Card: Angel's Breath / Archangel's Breath
Effect: powerful healing card in Greed Island.

## Card: Accompany
Effect: transports a player and companions to another specified player or location within Greed Island rules.

## Card: Leave
Effect: allows a player to exit Greed Island.
