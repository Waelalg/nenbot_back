# Supporting Characters

## Entity: Illumi Zoldyck
Type: Character
Aliases: Illumi, Illumi Zoldyck
Related: Killua, Zoldyck Family, Manipulation
Summary:
Illumi is Killua's older brother and a manipulative assassin. He uses needle-based Manipulation Nen and tries to control Killua's choices.

## Entity: Silva Zoldyck
Type: Character
Aliases: Silva, Silva Zoldyck
Related: Killua, Zeno, Zoldyck Family
Summary:
Silva is Killua's father and the head of the Zoldyck family. He is a powerful assassin with destructive aura attacks.

## Entity: Zeno Zoldyck
Type: Character
Aliases: Zeno, Zeno Zoldyck
Related: Dragon Dive, Silva, Killua
Summary:
Zeno is Killua's grandfather and an elite assassin. He uses dragon-shaped aura techniques including Dragon Dive.

## Entity: Kalluto Zoldyck
