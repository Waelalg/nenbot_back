# Hunters and the Hunter Association

## Entity: Hunter
Type: Profession
Aliases: Hunter, Hunters, licensed Hunter
Related: Hunter Association, Hunter Exam, Hunter License, Nen

Summary:
In Hunter x Hunter, a Hunter is a licensed elite professional recognized by the Hunter Association. Hunters pursue difficult, dangerous, or rare objectives such as finding unknown creatures, locating treasure, surveying unexplored places, capturing criminals, gathering information, or researching unusual phenomena.

Key facts:
- A Hunter license is granted after passing the Hunter Exam.
- Hunters receive major privileges and access to restricted information.
- Hunters often specialize in fields such as treasure, beasts, criminals, food, ruins, medicine, or exploration.
- Many skilled Hunters learn Nen, though the public does not fully understand it.

Common questions:
- What is a Hunter?
- Describe hunter for me.
- What does a Hunter license do?
- What is the Hunter Association?

## Entity: Hunter License
A Hunter license is proof that someone passed the Hunter Exam. It is extremely valuable and gives access to restricted places and opportunities.

## Entity: Hunter Exam
The Hunter Exam tests survival, intelligence, stamina, combat ability, judgment, and adaptability. Gon, Killua, Kurapika, and Leorio meet during the exam.

## Entity: Hunter Association
The Hunter Association is the organization responsible for testing, licensing, and organizing Hunters. Isaac Netero served as chairman for many years.

## Types of Hunters
Examples include Beast Hunters, Treasure Hunters, Blacklist Hunters, Gourmet Hunters, Archaeological Hunters, and medical or research-focused Hunters.

## Chairman Election
After Netero's death, the Hunter Association holds an election. The Zodiacs, Ging, Pariston, Cheadle, and many Hunters become involved.
