# 13th Hunter Chairman Election Arc

## Arc: Chairman Election
Summary:
After Netero's death, the Hunter Association holds an election. The arc also follows Killua's attempt to save Gon through Alluka and Nanika.

Key facts:
- Pariston and Cheadle are central election figures.
- Ging becomes more visible.
- Killua confronts family pressure while protecting Alluka.
- Nanika's power heals Gon.

Why it matters:
The arc resolves Gon's condition, develops Killua's independence, and changes Hunter Association leadership.
