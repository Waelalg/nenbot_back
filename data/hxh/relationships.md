# Relationships

## Relationship: Gon Freecss and Killua Zoldyck
Type: Friendship / main partnership
Summary:
Gon and Killua become close friends during the Hunter Exam. Gon gives Killua emotional freedom from his assassin family background, while Killua becomes Gon's loyal partner and protector.
Key points:
- They meet during the Hunter Exam.
- Killua values Gon's honesty and courage.
- Their friendship is central to several arcs.
- Their relationship becomes strained during the Chimera Ant arc because of Gon's emotional collapse.

## Relationship: Gon Freecss and Ging Freecss
Type: Father and son
Summary:
Ging is Gon's father and the reason Gon becomes a Hunter. Gon wants to understand why being a Hunter matters so much to Ging.

## Relationship: Gon Freecss and Kite
Type: Mentor connection
Summary:
Kite connects Gon to Ging's world and becomes emotionally important to Gon, especially in the Chimera Ant arc.

## Relationship: Killua and Illumi
Type: Family control
Summary:
Illumi manipulates Killua and represents the Zoldyck family's control over him.

## Relationship: Killua and Alluka/Nanika
Type: Sibling bond and protection
Summary:
Killua deeply cares for Alluka and Nanika and rejects the family's treatment of them as tools.

## Relationship: Kurapika and Phantom Troupe
Type: Revenge conflict
Summary:
Kurapika hates the Phantom Troupe because they massacred the Kurta Clan and stole the Scarlet Eyes.

## Relationship: Kurapika and Kurta Clan
Type: Survivor and heritage
Summary:
Kurapika carries the burden of being the last known survivor and wants to recover his clan's eyes.

## Relationship: Hisoka and Chrollo
Type: Rivalry
Summary:
Hisoka is fascinated by fighting Chrollo, while Chrollo treats conflict strategically.

## Relationship: Chrollo and Phantom Troupe
Type: Leader and group
Summary:
Chrollo leads the Troupe and is deeply tied to its identity.

## Relationship: Meruem and Komugi
Type: Transformative bond
Summary:
Komugi changes Meruem's view of strength, value, and humanity.

## Relationship: Netero and Meruem
Type: Opposing peaks
Summary:
Netero represents human training and resolve; Meruem represents overwhelming Chimera Ant evolution.

## Relationship: Biscuit with Gon and Killua
Type: Teacher and students
Summary:
Biscuit trains Gon and Killua in Greed Island and helps them mature as Nen users.

## Relationship: Leorio and Kurapika
Type: Friendship
Summary:
Leorio cares about Kurapika and the main group, often grounding them emotionally.
