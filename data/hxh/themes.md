# Themes

## Theme: Friendship
Gon and Killua's friendship is one of the emotional centers of the story. It gives Killua freedom but is tested by Gon's emotional extremes.

## Theme: Revenge
Kurapika represents revenge and the cost of dedicating life to a painful mission.

## Theme: Identity
Characters such as Killua, Meruem, and Kurapika struggle with who they are versus what others expect them to be.

## Theme: Humanity and Monstrosity
The Chimera Ant arc questions whether humans or monsters are more cruel and whether compassion can appear in unexpected places.

## Theme: Power and Sacrifice
Nen restrictions show that power often comes with cost, risk, and consequences.

## Theme: Morality
Hunter x Hunter often avoids simple heroes and villains. Many characters have mixed motives.

## Theme: Family Control
Killua's relationship with the Zoldycks shows control, pressure, and the struggle for independence.

## Theme: Freedom
Many characters seek freedom: Gon through adventure, Killua from family control, and Kurapika from grief.

## Theme: Ambition
Hunters often pursue extreme goals, from exploration to revenge to political power.

## Theme: Consequences of Nen Restrictions
Strong Nen vows can create huge power but also severe costs, as seen with Kurapika and Gon.
