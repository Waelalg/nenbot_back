# Factions and Groups

## Group: Hunter Association
The Hunter Association tests, licenses, and organizes professional Hunters. It is influential because Hunters operate in dangerous and restricted areas.

## Group: Phantom Troupe
The Phantom Troupe, also called the Spiders, is a criminal group from Meteor City led by Chrollo Lucilfer. The group is tied to the Kurta Clan massacre and Kurapika's revenge.

## Group: Zoldyck Family
The Zoldyck family is a famous assassin family living on Kukuroo Mountain. Important members include Zeno, Silva, Illumi, Killua, Alluka, and Kalluto.

## Group: Chimera Ants
Chimera Ants inherit traits from what the Queen consumes. Their hierarchy includes soldiers, squadron leaders, Royal Guards, and the King.

## Group: Royal Guards
The Royal Guards protect Meruem. The guards are Neferpitou, Shaiapouf, and Menthuthuyoupi.

## Group: Zodiacs
The Zodiacs are elite Hunters connected to Hunter Association leadership. They become important after Netero's death.

## Group: Greed Island Game Masters
The Game Masters created and maintain Greed Island through Nen. Ging is one of the creators.

## Group: Mafia Community
The mafia community is prominent in Yorknew City and clashes with the Phantom Troupe during the underground auction.

## Group: Kurta Clan
The Kurta Clan was massacred for their Scarlet Eyes. Kurapika is the last known survivor.

## Group: Kakin Empire
The Kakin Empire sponsors the Dark Continent expedition and runs the Succession Contest among its princes.

## Group: Princes of Kakin
The princes compete in a deadly succession war aboard the Black Whale, each influenced by Guardian Spirit Beasts.

## Group: Heavens Arena Fighters
Heavens Arena fighters compete in a combat tower where Nen becomes essential at higher levels.
