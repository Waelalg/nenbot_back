# Major Events

The Hunter Exam brings Gon, Killua, Kurapika, and Leorio together. It also introduces Hisoka as a dangerous recurring figure.

Kurapika's conflict with Uvogin in Yorknew City shows the power of Nen restrictions and Kurapika's resolve against the Phantom Troupe.

Chrollo's Nen is sealed by Kurapika during the Yorknew City arc, changing the Phantom Troupe's situation and forcing them to seek a way to restore their leader's abilities.

Gon and Killua's training with Biscuit on Greed Island greatly improves their Nen control and combat thinking.

Kite's confrontation with Neferpitou leads to one of the most important emotional turning points for Gon.

The palace invasion in the Chimera Ant arc brings Hunters into direct conflict with Meruem and the Royal Guards. Netero fights Meruem, while Gon, Killua, Knuckle, Shoot, Morel, Knov, and others handle different parts of the operation.

Meruem's bond with Komugi changes his understanding of strength and humanity. Their relationship is central to the conclusion of the Chimera Ant arc.

Killua uses Alluka and Nanika's power during the Election arc to save Gon after the consequences of Gon's transformation.
