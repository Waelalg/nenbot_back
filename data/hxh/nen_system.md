# Nen System

## Topic: Nen
Type: Power system
Aliases: Nen, aura, Hatsu, Ten, Zetsu, Ren
Related: Six Nen categories, Water Divination, vows, restrictions

Summary:
Nen is the practice of controlling aura, the life energy produced by living beings. It is the central combat and ability system in Hunter x Hunter.

Key facts:
- Aura is life energy.
- Nen users learn to open and control aura nodes.
- The four basic principles are Ten, Zetsu, Ren, and Hatsu.
- The six categories are Enhancement, Transmutation, Emission, Conjuration, Manipulation, and Specialization.
- Restrictions and vows can make abilities stronger by adding risk or limits.

## Principle: Ten
Ten keeps aura around the body. It protects the user, slows aura leakage, and forms the foundation for stable Nen use.

## Principle: Zetsu
Zetsu suppresses aura output and presence. It helps conceal the user but makes them vulnerable if attacked.

## Principle: Ren
Ren increases aura output. It strengthens presence, offense, and defense, but it consumes more stamina.

## Principle: Hatsu
Hatsu is the expression of a user's personal Nen. It is how aura becomes a unique ability or effect.

## Category: Enhancement
Enhancement strengthens the body or objects. Gon is associated with Enhancement.

## Category: Transmutation
Transmutation changes aura properties. Killua uses electricity-like aura, and Hisoka gives aura rubber and gum properties.

## Category: Emission
Emission projects aura away from the body. Leorio's remote punch is an example.

## Category: Conjuration
Conjuration creates objects from aura. Kurapika's chains are a major example.

## Category: Manipulation
Manipulation controls people, objects, or conditions. Illumi's needle control is an example.

## Category: Specialization
Specialization covers abilities that do not fit the other categories. Chrollo's Skill Hunter and Kurapika's Emperor Time are linked to Specialization.

## Water Divination
Water Divination is a test used to identify a person's natural Nen category by observing how aura changes water and a leaf.

## Vows and Limitations
Nen can become stronger when a user accepts strict rules. Kurapika's Chain Jail is powerful because it is limited to Phantom Troupe members.
