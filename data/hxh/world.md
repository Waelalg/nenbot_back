# World and Lore

Hunter x Hunter takes place in a world where licensed Hunters receive special privileges to explore dangerous places, access restricted information, capture criminals, discover treasure, and investigate unknown phenomena.

Whale Island is Gon's home. It is where he grows up before leaving to take the Hunter Exam and search for Ging.

Meteor City is a lawless and isolated place connected to the origins of several Phantom Troupe members. Its people are often treated as nonexistent by outside society.

Kukuroo Mountain is the location of the Zoldyck family estate. The estate is heavily guarded and symbolizes the danger and discipline surrounding Killua's family.

Greed Island is a Nen-created game world. It looks like a video game but is a real place operating under complex Nen rules and card systems.

The NGL region becomes important in the Chimera Ant arc. It is isolated and rejects modern technology, which helps the Chimera Ant threat grow before the wider world understands the danger.

The Dark Continent is an extremely dangerous and mysterious land beyond the known world. It is associated with immense risk, unknown creatures, and potential rewards.
