# World and Locations

## Location: Whale Island
Whale Island is Gon's home. Mito raises him there before he leaves for the Hunter Exam.

## Location: Kukuroo Mountain
Kukuroo Mountain contains the Zoldyck family estate, guarded by gates, servants, and trained assassins.

## Location: Yorknew City
Yorknew City is the setting for the mafia auction and Kurapika's conflict with the Phantom Troupe.

## Location: Heavens Arena
Heavens Arena is a massive combat tower where fighters earn money and rank up through battles. It introduces Nen formally.

## Location: Greed Island
Greed Island is a Nen-created game world connected to Ging Freecss. It uses cards, spells, and restricted slots.

## Location: NGL
NGL is an isolated region where the Chimera Ant threat grows. Its rejection of modern technology helps the crisis spread unnoticed.

## Location: East Gorteau
East Gorteau becomes important during the Chimera Ant palace invasion and Meruem's rule.

## Location: Meteor City
Meteor City is isolated and tied to the Phantom Troupe's origins.

## Location: Dark Continent
The Dark Continent is a dangerous land beyond the known world, associated with extreme risks and unknown rewards.

## Location: Black Whale
The Black Whale is the ship carrying Kakin's royal family and expedition forces toward the Dark Continent.

## Location: Kakin Empire
The Kakin Empire drives the Succession Contest and the Dark Continent expedition.
