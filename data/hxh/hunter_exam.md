# Hunter Exam Arc

## Arc: Hunter Exam
Summary:
The Hunter Exam introduces Gon, Killua, Kurapika, Leorio, Hisoka, Hanzo, Tonpa, Pokkle, Ponzu, Menchi, Buhara, and Satotz. Candidates face endurance tests, cooking tests, trick tower challenges, deception, combat, and final evaluations.

Key facts:
- Gon joins to become a Hunter and find Ging.
- Killua appears as a talented but isolated assassin child.
- Kurapika seeks power and access to pursue the Phantom Troupe.
- Leorio wants resources and status to become a doctor.
- Hisoka is introduced as a dangerous competitor.

Why it matters:
The arc forms the core friendships and establishes that Hunter society is dangerous and selective.
