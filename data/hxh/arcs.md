# Story Arcs

## Arc: Hunter Exam
Summary:
Gon, Killua, Kurapika, and Leorio meet while attempting to become licensed Hunters. The exam tests survival, intelligence, endurance, deception, and combat.
Main characters: Gon, Killua, Kurapika, Leorio, Hisoka, Hanzo.
Conflict: candidates compete in dangerous tests.
Why it matters: introduces the main group, Hunter licenses, and the dangerous world.

## Arc: Zoldyck Family
Summary:
Gon, Kurapika, and Leorio go to the Zoldyck estate to bring Killua back. The arc explores Killua's assassin family and his desire for friendship.
Main characters: Killua, Gon, Silva, Zeno, Canary.
Why it matters: establishes the Zoldyck family and Killua's conflict with family control.

## Arc: Heavens Arena
Summary:
Gon and Killua climb a combat tower and formally learn Nen from Wing. Hisoka becomes a major opponent.
Main characters: Gon, Killua, Wing, Zushi, Hisoka.
Important concepts: Nen, Ten, Zetsu, Ren, Hatsu.
Why it matters: introduces the power system.

## Arc: Yorknew City
Summary:
Kurapika hunts the Phantom Troupe while the mafia world and the Troupe collide over an underground auction.
Main characters: Kurapika, Chrollo, Phantom Troupe, Gon, Killua, Leorio.
Important events: Kurapika defeats Uvogin, captures Chrollo, and seals Chrollo's Nen.
Why it matters: develops Kurapika's revenge and the Phantom Troupe.

## Arc: Greed Island
Summary:
Gon and Killua enter a Nen-created game tied to Ging. Biscuit trains them while they learn card mechanics and face Genthru's group.
Main characters: Gon, Killua, Biscuit, Genthru, Razor.
Important concepts: spell cards, restricted slots, Accompany, Leave, Angel's Breath.
Why it matters: advances Gon and Killua's Nen training and Ging search.

## Arc: Chimera Ant
Summary:
Chimera Ants evolve into a massive threat. Meruem is born as king, protected by Royal Guards. Hunters launch a palace invasion.
Main characters: Meruem, Komugi, Netero, Gon, Killua, Kite, Neferpitou, Shaiapouf, Youpi.
Important events: Kite's fate, palace invasion, Netero vs Meruem, Gon vs Pitou, Meruem and Komugi's bond.
Why it matters: explores humanity, power, sacrifice, and morality.

## Arc: 13th Hunter Chairman Election
Summary:
After Netero's death, the Hunter Association elects a new chairman. Killua uses Alluka and Nanika's power to save Gon.
Main characters: Killua, Alluka, Nanika, Gon, Ging, Pariston, Cheadle.
Why it matters: resolves Gon's condition and shifts Hunter Association leadership.

## Arc: Dark Continent Expedition
Summary:
The Kakin Empire and Beyond Netero prepare for a dangerous journey connected to the Dark Continent.
Main characters: Beyond Netero, Ging, Pariston, Zodiacs, Kakin officials.
Why it matters: expands the world beyond the known map.

## Arc: Succession Contest
Summary:
Kakin princes compete in a deadly succession war aboard the Black Whale. Kurapika protects Prince Woble and Queen Oito.
Main characters: Kurapika, Woble, Oito, Kakin princes, Phantom Troupe, Hisoka.
Important concepts: Guardian Spirit Beasts, Black Whale, Kakin politics.
Why it matters: combines political strategy, Nen mysteries, and Dark Continent setup.
