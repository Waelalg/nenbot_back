# Heavens Arena

## Arc: Heavens Arena
Summary:
Heavens Arena is a combat tower where Gon and Killua train and fight. The arc formally introduces Nen through Wing and Zushi.

Key facts:
- Gon and Killua learn Ten, Zetsu, Ren, and Hatsu.
- Wing teaches Nen carefully after the boys are exposed to aura pressure.
- Hisoka blocks their progress until they can use Nen.
- Gon fights Hisoka and begins to understand the gap between talent and mastery.

Why it matters:
This arc explains the power system that shapes the rest of the series.
