# Greed Island

## Topic: Greed Island
Type: Arc and game system
Aliases: Greed Island, Green Island
Related: Gon, Killua, Ging, Biscuit, Razor, cards, spell cards

Summary:
Greed Island is a Nen-created game world designed by Ging Freecss and other Game Masters. Gon and Killua enter it to search for clues about Ging and improve their Nen.

Key facts:
- Greed Island looks like a game but takes place in a real Nen-controlled environment.
- Players collect cards in restricted slots.
- Spell cards create effects such as movement, copying, or healing.
- Biscuit trains Gon and Killua during the arc.
- Razor is a powerful Game Master connected to a dodgeball challenge.
- Genthru is a major antagonist in the arc.

## Game Mechanics
Players use binders to store cards. Some cards are numbered restricted-slot cards needed to complete the game. Spell cards support movement, tracking, copying, and strategy.

## Important Cards
Leave allows a player to exit the game. Accompany transports players under game rules. Angel's Breath, also called Archangel's Breath, is a powerful healing card.

## Training
Biscuit teaches Gon and Killua advanced Nen basics, combat awareness, and efficient aura control.
