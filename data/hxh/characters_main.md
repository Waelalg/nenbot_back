# Main Characters

## Entity: Gon Freecss
Type: Character
Aliases: Gon, Gon Freecss, Gon Freaks, Gon Freeks
Related: Killua Zoldyck, Ging Freecss, Kite, Jajanken, Enhancement

Summary:
Gon Freecss is the main protagonist. He is an optimistic boy from Whale Island who becomes a Hunter to find his father, Ging Freecss.

Key facts:
- Best friend and traveling partner of Killua.
- Nen affinity: Enhancement.
- Main ability: Jajanken.
- Important arcs: Hunter Exam, Heavens Arena, Greed Island, Chimera Ant.
- His emotional reaction to Kite's fate is central to the Chimera Ant arc.

- Who is Gon?
- What is Gon's Nen type?
- What is Jajanken?
- Who is Ging to Gon?

## Entity: Killua Zoldyck
Type: Character
Aliases: Killua, Kilua, Killwa, Killua Zoldyck
Related: Gon Freecss, Zoldyck Family, Alluka, Nanika, Godspeed, Transmutation

Summary:
Killua Zoldyck is one of the main characters. He is a young assassin from the Zoldyck family and becomes Gon Freecss's closest friend.

Key facts:
- Member of the Zoldyck family.
- Best friend and traveling partner of Gon.
- Nen affinity: Transmutation.
- Uses electricity-based abilities such as Godspeed.
- Important in Hunter Exam, Heavens Arena, Greed Island, Chimera Ant, and Election arcs.

Common questions this section should answer:
- Who is Killua?
- What is Killua's Nen type?
- What is Godspeed?
- What is Killua's relationship with Gon?

## Entity: Kurapika
Type: Character
Aliases: Kurapika, Kurapica, Kurapicka
Related: Kurta Clan, Phantom Troupe, Scarlet Eyes, Chain Jail, Emperor Time

Summary:
Kurapika is the last known survivor of the Kurta Clan. He becomes a Hunter to recover the Scarlet Eyes and pursue the Phantom Troupe.

Key facts:
- His Scarlet Eyes activate Emperor Time.
- Uses conjured chains with different functions.
- Chain Jail is restricted to Phantom Troupe members.
- Central to Yorknew City and Succession Contest.

Common questions:
- Who is Kurapika?
- Why does Kurapika hate the Phantom Troupe?
- What is Emperor Time?
- How does Chain Jail work?

## Entity: Leorio Paradinight
Type: Character
Aliases: Leorio, Leorio Paradinight
Related: Gon, Killua, Kurapika, Hunter Exam, medicine

Summary:
Leorio is one of Gon's first companions. He wants to become a doctor and uses the Hunter path to support that goal.

Key facts:
- Loyal, emotional, and practical.
- Wants to help people who cannot afford treatment.
- Later demonstrates an Emission-style remote punch.
- Important in Hunter Exam and Election arcs.

## Entity: Hisoka Morow
Type: Character
Aliases: Hisoka, Hisuca, Hisoka Morow
Related: Bungee Gum, Texture Surprise, Gon, Phantom Troupe

Summary:
Hisoka is a dangerous magician-like fighter obsessed with strong opponents and future potential.

Key facts:
- Nen affinity: Transmutation.
- Main ability: Bungee Gum.
- Uses Texture Surprise for deception.
- Takes interest in Gon and Killua because of their growth.

## Entity: Chrollo Lucilfer
Type: Character
Aliases: Chrollo, Chrolo, Kuroro, Chrollo Lucilfer
Related: Phantom Troupe, Skill Hunter, Yorknew City

Summary:
Chrollo Lucilfer is the calm and strategic leader of the Phantom Troupe.

Key facts:
- Leads the Spiders.
- Ability: Skill Hunter, which steals and stores Nen abilities under conditions.
- Important in Yorknew City and Phantom Troupe conflicts.

## Entity: Isaac Netero
Type: Character
Aliases: Netero, Isaac Netero
Related: Hunter Association, 100-Type Guanyin Bodhisattva, Meruem

Summary:
Isaac Netero is the former chairman of the Hunter Association and one of the strongest known Nen users.

Key facts:
- Uses 100-Type Guanyin Bodhisattva.
- Represents the peak of human training.
- Fights Meruem during the Chimera Ant arc.

## Entity: Ging Freecss
Type: Character
Aliases: Ging, Ging Freecss
Related: Gon Freecss, Greed Island, Hunter Association

Summary:
Ging Freecss is Gon's father and a famous Hunter. Gon becomes a Hunter partly to find him.

Key facts:
- One of the creators of Greed Island.
- Known as an exceptional and difficult-to-understand Hunter.
- Important to Gon's motivation and the Election arc.

## Entity: Kite
Type: Character
Aliases: Kite
Related: Ging Freecss, Gon Freecss, Crazy Slots, Chimera Ant

Summary:
Kite is a Hunter trained by Ging. He reconnects Gon to Ging's world and investigates the Chimera Ant threat.

Key facts:
- Ability: Crazy Slots.
- His encounter with Neferpitou deeply affects Gon.

## Entity: Biscuit Krueger
Type: Character
Aliases: Biscuit, Bisky, Biscuit Krueger
Related: Greed Island, Gon, Killua, Nen training

Summary:
Biscuit Krueger is a veteran Hunter and Nen teacher who trains Gon and Killua during Greed Island.

Key facts:
- Expert in Nen fundamentals and combat training.
- Usually appears as a small girl but has a powerful true form.

## Entity: Alluka Zoldyck
Type: Character
Aliases: Alluka, Alluka Zoldyck
Related: Nanika, Killua, Zoldyck Family

Summary:
Alluka is a member of the Zoldyck family and is closely connected to Nanika, a mysterious being with wish-granting power.

## Entity: Nanika
Type: Entity
Aliases: Nanika
Related: Alluka Zoldyck, Killua, wish granting

Summary:
Nanika can grant wishes after requests are fulfilled. The power is extremely dangerous under normal conditions, but Killua has a special bond with Alluka and Nanika.

## Entity: Meruem
Type: Character
Aliases: Meruem, Merum, Mereum
Related: Chimera Ants, Komugi, Royal Guards, Netero

Summary:
Meruem is the Chimera Ant King. He begins as a ruler defined by power but changes through his relationship with Komugi.

## Entity: Neferpitou
Type: Character
Aliases: Neferpitou, Pitou
Related: Royal Guards, Meruem, Doctor Blythe, Terpsichora, Kite, Gon

Summary:
Neferpitou is one of Meruem's Royal Guards. Pitou has terrifying aura and abilities connected to healing and puppet-like combat.

## Entity: Shaiapouf
Type: Character
Aliases: Shaiapouf, Pouf
Related: Royal Guards, Meruem, Spiritual Message

Summary:
Shaiapouf is a Royal Guard who is fanatically devoted to Meruem as king. He is manipulative, theatrical, and strategic.

## Entity: Menthuthuyoupi
Type: Character
Aliases: Menthuthuyoupi, Youpi
Related: Royal Guards, Meruem, body transformation

Summary:
Menthuthuyoupi is a Royal Guard with enormous physical power and a body that can transform during combat.
