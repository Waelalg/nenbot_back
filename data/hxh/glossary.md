# Glossary

## Aura
Life energy controlled through Nen.

## Nen
The practice of controlling aura.

## Ten
Maintaining aura around the body.

## Zetsu
Suppressing aura and presence.

## Ren
Increasing aura output.

## Hatsu
Expressing a personal Nen ability.

## Gyo
Focusing aura into a body part, often the eyes.

## En
Expanding aura as a detection field.

## Ko
Focusing most aura into one point.

## Ken
Maintaining strong aura around the body.

## Ryu
Changing aura distribution in real time.

## Hunter
A licensed elite professional recognized by the Hunter Association.

## Hunter License
Proof of passing the Hunter Exam and a source of major privileges.

## Scarlet Eyes
The Kurta Clan's rare eyes that turn scarlet under strong emotion.

## Royal Guards
Meruem's three protectors: Neferpitou, Shaiapouf, and Menthuthuyoupi.

## Spider
Nickname for the Phantom Troupe.
