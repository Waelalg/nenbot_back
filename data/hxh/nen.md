# Nen

Nen is the system in Hunter x Hunter that lets living beings control aura, their life energy. It is used for combat, detection, protection, healing, strategy, and specialized abilities. Nen is dangerous because abilities often become stronger through restrictions, vows, and personal risk.

## Four Major Principles
Ten keeps aura around the body and improves defense.

Zetsu suppresses aura and presence, making the user harder to sense but more vulnerable.

Ren increases aura output and strengthens presence, defense, and offense.

Hatsu expresses a user's personal Nen ability based on personality, training, and category affinity.

## Six Nen Categories
Enhancement strengthens the body or objects. Gon is associated with Enhancement.

Emission projects aura away from the body. Leorio's remote punch is an example of Emission.

Manipulation controls people, objects, or conditions. Illumi's needle control is an example.

Transmutation changes aura properties. Killua changes aura into electricity, and Hisoka gives aura rubber and gum properties.

Conjuration creates objects from aura. Kurapika's chains are a major example.

Specialization covers abilities that do not fit the other categories. Chrollo's Skill Hunter and Kurapika's Emperor Time are associated with Specialization.

## Restrictions and Vows
Nen abilities can become stronger when the user accepts strict limits. Kurapika's Chain Jail is powerful because it is restricted to Phantom Troupe members. This principle makes Nen battles strategic instead of simply based on raw power.

## Explaining Nen Simply
Nen is like controlled life energy. Basic training teaches a user to keep aura close, hide it, increase it, and shape it into a personal ability. The best abilities match the user's nature and often include conditions that create power in exchange for risk.
