# Hunter x Hunter Overview

## Topic: Hunter x Hunter
Type: Series overview
Aliases: HxH, Hunter x Hunter, Hunter Hunter
Related: Gon Freecss, Ging Freecss, Hunter Exam, Nen, Hunter Association

Summary:
Hunter x Hunter is a manga and anime series created by Yoshihiro Togashi. The story begins with Gon Freecss leaving Whale Island to become a licensed Hunter and search for his father, Ging Freecss. The series grows from an adventure story into a world of dangerous exams, criminal groups, strategic Nen battles, political conflict, and moral dilemmas.

Key facts:
- Created by Yoshihiro Togashi.
- Began as a manga and has anime adaptations.
- Gon wants to become a Hunter and find Ging.
- Hunters are licensed elite professionals who take on rare, dangerous, or difficult missions.
- Nen is the main supernatural power system.
- The world is dangerous because Hunters face criminals, monsters, unknown lands, and other Nen users.

## Definition: Hunter
In Hunter x Hunter, a Hunter is not just a person who hunts animals. A Hunter is a licensed professional recognized by the Hunter Association. Hunters may search for treasure, criminals, rare creatures, ruins, ingredients, lost people, medical knowledge, or unexplored places. A Hunter license gives access to privileges and restricted information.

## Core Premise
Gon Freecss learns that his absent father Ging is a famous Hunter. Gon decides to take the Hunter Exam, meet other candidates, and enter the wider world. His friendships with Killua, Kurapika, and Leorio shape the early story.
