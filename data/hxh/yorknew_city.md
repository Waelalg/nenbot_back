# Yorknew City Arc

## Arc: Yorknew City
Summary:
Yorknew City focuses on Kurapika's revenge, the Phantom Troupe, the mafia auction, and Chrollo's leadership.

Key facts:
- The Phantom Troupe attacks the underground auction.
- Kurapika captures and kills Uvogin.
- Kurapika uses Chain Jail and Judgment Chain.
- Chrollo's Nen is sealed under Kurapika's conditions.
- Gon and Killua are pulled into the conflict.

Why it matters:
The arc develops Kurapika, explains the Phantom Troupe's danger, and shows Nen as a battle of information and conditions.
