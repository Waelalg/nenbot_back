# Common Questions and Answers

## Q: Who is Killua?
A: Killua Zoldyck is one of the main characters. He is a young assassin from the Zoldyck family, Gon's closest friend, and a Transmutation Nen user who develops electricity-based abilities such as Godspeed.

## Q: What is Nen?
A: Nen is the practice of controlling aura, the life energy of living beings. Its basics are Ten, Zetsu, Ren, and Hatsu, and abilities are shaped by six categories.

## Q: List all Nen types.
A: The six Nen categories are Enhancement, Transmutation, Emission, Conjuration, Manipulation, and Specialization.

## Q: Difference between Ten and Ren?
A: Ten keeps aura stable around the body for protection, while Ren increases aura output for stronger offense, defense, and presence.

## Q: What happened in the Chimera Ant arc?
A: Chimera Ants evolve into a major threat, Meruem is born as king, the Royal Guards protect him, Hunters invade the palace, and the arc centers on battles such as Netero vs Meruem and Gon vs Pitou.

## Q: Who are the Phantom Troupe?
A: The Phantom Troupe are a criminal group from Meteor City led by Chrollo Lucilfer. They are also called the Spiders and are central to Kurapika's revenge.

## Q: What is Bungee Gum?
A: Bungee Gum is Hisoka's Transmutation ability. It gives aura properties of both rubber and gum.

## Q: What is Greed Island?
A: Greed Island is a Nen-created game world connected to Ging. Gon and Killua train there, collect cards, and search for clues about Ging.

## Q: Describe hunter for me.
A: In Hunter x Hunter, a Hunter is a licensed elite professional who passes the Hunter Exam and takes on rare, dangerous, or specialized missions.

## Q: Who is Gon?
A: Gon Freecss is the main protagonist. He becomes a Hunter to find his father Ging and is associated with Enhancement and Jajanken.

## Q: Who is Kurapika?
A: Kurapika is the last known survivor of the Kurta Clan. He hunts the Phantom Troupe and uses chain-based Nen abilities.

## Q: Who is Hisoka?
A: Hisoka Morow is a dangerous fighter who loves strong opponents. His main Nen ability is Bungee Gum.

## Q: Who is Chrollo?
A: Chrollo Lucilfer is the leader of the Phantom Troupe and uses Skill Hunter to steal Nen abilities.

## Q: Who is Meruem?
A: Meruem is the Chimera Ant King. His relationship with Komugi changes his view of power and humanity.

## Q: Who is Komugi?
A: Komugi is a blind Gungi champion whose bond with Meruem is central to the Chimera Ant arc.

## Q: Who is Netero?
A: Isaac Netero is the former Hunter Association chairman and one of the strongest Nen users shown.

## Q: What is Hatsu?
A: Hatsu is the personal expression of a Nen user's aura, often becoming a unique ability.

## Q: What is Ten?
A: Ten keeps aura around the body for stability and protection.

## Q: What is Zetsu?
A: Zetsu suppresses aura and presence, helping conceal the user while reducing defense.

## Q: What is Ren?
A: Ren increases aura output for stronger presence, offense, and defense.

## Q: What is Gyo?
A: Gyo focuses aura into a body part, often the eyes, to see hidden aura or strengthen a point.

## Q: What is En?
A: En expands aura around the body as a detection field.

## Q: What is Ko?
A: Ko concentrates most aura into one point for maximum attack or defense.

## Q: What is Ken?
A: Ken maintains a strong defensive aura around the whole body.

## Q: What is Ryu?
A: Ryu shifts aura distribution during combat for flexible offense and defense.

## Q: What is post-mortem Nen?
A: Post-mortem Nen is aura that remains or strengthens after death because of intense emotion or conditions.

## Q: What is Greed Island?
A: Greed Island is a Nen-created game world connected to Ging, cards, Game Masters, and Gon and Killua's training.

## Q: What are Greed Island cards?
A: Greed Island cards are Nen-based game items and spells stored in a binder. They control movement, healing, copying, and game progress.

## Q: What is Accompany?
A: Accompany is a Greed Island spell card that transports players under game rules.

## Q: What is Leave?
A: Leave is a Greed Island card that lets a player exit the game.

## Q: What is Angel's Breath?
A: Angel's Breath is a powerful Greed Island healing card.

## Q: Summarize Yorknew City.
A: Yorknew City focuses on Kurapika's revenge, the Phantom Troupe, the mafia auction, and Chrollo's capture.

## Q: Which arc introduces Nen?
A: Heavens Arena formally introduces Nen through Wing's training of Gon and Killua.

## Q: List arcs in order.
A: Hunter Exam, Zoldyck Family, Heavens Arena, Yorknew City, Greed Island, Chimera Ant, Chairman Election, Dark Continent Expedition, Succession Contest.

## Q: Who are the Royal Guards?
A: The Royal Guards are Neferpitou, Shaiapouf, and Menthuthuyoupi, who protect Meruem.

## Q: What is the Hunter Association?
A: The Hunter Association licenses Hunters, runs the Hunter Exam, and organizes professional Hunters.

## Q: What is the Zoldyck family?
A: The Zoldyck family is a famous assassin family from Kukuroo Mountain.

## Q: What is the Kurta Clan?
A: The Kurta Clan is Kurapika's clan, known for Scarlet Eyes and massacred before the main story.

## Q: What is the Dark Continent?
A: The Dark Continent is a dangerous land beyond the known world tied to extreme risks and exploration.

## Q: What is the Succession Contest?
A: The Succession Contest is a deadly Kakin royal conflict aboard the Black Whale.

## Q: What is Emperor Time?
A: Emperor Time is Kurapika's Scarlet Eyes state that lets him use all Nen categories at full efficiency at a severe cost.

## Q: What is Chain Jail?
A: Chain Jail is Kurapika's chain ability that restrains Phantom Troupe members and forces them into Zetsu.

## Q: What is Skill Hunter?
A: Skill Hunter is Chrollo's ability to steal and use other people's Nen abilities under conditions.

## Q: What is Jajanken?
A: Jajanken is Gon's charged Nen attack with Rock, Paper, and Scissors variations.

## Q: How does Godspeed work?
A: Godspeed uses Killua's electricity-like aura to move and react extremely fast.

## Q: How does Nanika wish granting work?
A: Nanika can grant wishes through Alluka after request conditions, but the power is normally dangerous.

## Q: Compare Phantom Troupe and Hunter Association.
A: The Hunter Association is a legal professional organization, while the Phantom Troupe is a criminal group from Meteor City.

## Q: Compare Ten and Ren.
A: Ten stabilizes aura around the body; Ren increases aura output and intensity.

## Q: Compare Ko and Ken.
A: Ko concentrates aura into one point, while Ken maintains strong aura around the whole body.

## Q: Compare Gyo and En.
A: Gyo focuses aura into the eyes or a point, while En expands aura outward to detect surroundings.

## Q: What is Killua's relationship with Gon?
A: Killua is Gon's closest friend and partner. Their bond helps Killua seek freedom from his assassin upbringing.

## Q: Who is Ging to Gon?
A: Ging Freecss is Gon's father and the reason Gon becomes a Hunter.

## Q: What is Killua's relationship with Alluka?
A: Killua deeply cares for Alluka and protects her and Nanika from the Zoldyck family's control.

## Q: Why does Kurapika hate the Phantom Troupe?
A: Kurapika hates the Phantom Troupe because they massacred the Kurta Clan and stole the Scarlet Eyes.

## Q: Explain Meruem and Komugi.
A: Komugi changes Meruem's understanding of value and humanity through their Gungi games and emotional bond.

## Q: What themes does Kurapika represent?
A: Kurapika represents revenge, grief, loyalty to heritage, and the cost of obsession.

## Q: What is the message of the Chimera Ant arc?
A: The Chimera Ant arc explores humanity, monstrosity, power, compassion, and sacrifice.

## Q: Why is Hunter x Hunter complex?
A: It combines adventure with strategic Nen rules, moral ambiguity, political conflict, and character-driven consequences.
