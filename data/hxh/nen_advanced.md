# Advanced Nen

## Technique: Gyo
Gyo focuses aura into a specific body part, often the eyes. It helps detect hidden aura, including abilities concealed by In.

## Technique: In
In hides aura and makes Nen constructs or aura effects harder to see. Opponents often use Gyo to detect it.

## Technique: En
En expands aura outward as a detection field. It combines control and output to sense anything entering the aura radius.

## Technique: Shu
Shu extends aura to an object, strengthening or protecting it.

## Technique: Ko
Ko focuses almost all aura into one point for maximum offense or defense at that spot, leaving the rest of the body exposed.

## Technique: Ken
Ken maintains a strong layer of aura around the whole body. It is useful for sustained combat defense.

## Technique: Ryu
Ryu shifts aura percentages around the body in real time. It lets a Nen user adapt offense and defense during battle.

## Nen Contracts
Nen contracts are strict self-imposed conditions. The harsher the risk or limitation, the greater the possible power increase.

## Post-Mortem Nen
Post-mortem Nen is aura that becomes stronger or continues after death, usually because of intense emotion, obsession, or a powerful condition.

## Aura Nodes and Awakening
Nen awakening involves opening aura nodes. If done forcefully, it can be dangerous. Proper training teaches control before combat use.

## Nen Combat Strategy
Nen battles reward information, deception, conditions, category compatibility, and emotional discipline. Raw aura matters, but strategy often decides fights.
